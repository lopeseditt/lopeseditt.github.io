FAQ

Good to know

How fast is delivery? +

Usually between 24-72 hours. Short clips (Shorts, Reels, TikTok) usually turn around quickly. Longer YouTube edits take more time. Exact timelines get confirmed before I start.

How many revisions are included? +

A reasonable round of revisions is included with every edit. If something's off, we fix it.

What do you need from me? +

Your raw footage or audio, plus any references or style examples you want me to match.

How do we communicate? +

Mostly on Discord. Email and Telegram work too, whatever's easiest for you.

How do I get started? +

Message me on Discord with what you need edited, and we'll take it from there.
